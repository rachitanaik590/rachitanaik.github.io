About

I'm a Machine Learning Engineer passionate about building large-scale, real-time ML systems. At Lyft, I design and ship models that improve ride reliability, ETA accuracy, and driver-rider matching, impacting millions of rides each year.

Background

I hold a Master's degree in Computer Science (Machine Learning) from Columbia University and have published research in IEEE conferences on applied deep learning in healthcare. My focus lies in ML systems that thrive in production environments—from feature engineering and model training to deployment, monitoring, and iteration at scale.

Beyond Work

I enjoy writing and speaking about applied machine learning, scalable systems, and responsible AI—sharing practical lessons from building real-world ML products. In my free time, you'll find me exploring new AI ideas, reading and writing about emerging tech, traveling, or enjoying good food, usually while thinking through the next system I want to build.
